The Card game is a type of game that requires two players, each player rolls a dice and if the player rolls a ONE the players losses he's or her current score and it's the next player's turn. Also if a player rolls a SIX consecutively the player losses the entire score. A player can choose to hold the score from each die roll as long as it's not a ONE
